Private tour

Create your own unique, personal diving experience in Iceland. We’ll help you build a private individual or group tour. It offers us an opportunity to do what we love, experiencing the country’s beauty and sharing some of Iceland’s best kept secrets. We also carry extensive knowledge of dive sites along the northern and eastern parts of the country, as well as the breathtaking Westman Islands. Contact us and we’ll help you get started.

In addition, any of our current tours can be customized for private individual or small groups that wish to experience Iceland’s stunning underwater landscapes together.
