Diving is our passion

No one loves diving like we do! We are passionate divers who provide fun, safe, and educational tours around Iceland’s most breathtaking underwater scenery. With its hot springs, glaciers, and lava formations, the country maintains some of the most unique locations in the world. Our focus is on safety and comfort, and we only use high-quality, carefully curated and tested equipment specialized for the extreme arctic conditions.

Diving Island

Our team

Diving Island was founded by two close friends and local divers who decided to turn their dream of sharing their love for diving into a reality.

Héðinn Þorkelsson, Guide

Héðinn is a PADI Open Water Scuba and rebreather instructor. He has been exploring Iceland’ underwater for the last 16 years. He has been an active member of Iceland’s diving club since 2003, and has served as a member of its board of directors. As a diver, Héðinn loves taking pictures of underwater sites and wildlife. He is not only a scuba diving instructor but also a freediver, and holds the national record of 140 meters in dynamic diving.

Einar Örn Ágústsson, Guide

After his first dive in 2016, Einar decided to drop everything and make a career out of diving, so he could share his love of Iceland’s underwater nature with others. Since then, he has devoted himself to learning and exploring diving sites throughout Iceland. Einar is a certified PADI Open Water Scuba instructor and holds an Aida 2 certification in freediving.
